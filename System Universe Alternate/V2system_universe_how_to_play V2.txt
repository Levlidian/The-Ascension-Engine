SYSTEM UNIVERSE — HOW TO PLAY (UPDATED GUIDE)

---

## CORE IDEA

You do not select abilities or classes manually.

Everything is based on:

* What you do
* How consistently you do it
* How well you execute it

The System reads your actions and converts them into growth.

---

## HOW LEVELING WORKS

There are TWO types of progression:

---

1. PLAYER LEVEL (XP)

You gain XP through:

* Meaningful actions
* Combat
* Interaction and awareness
* Longer, more detailed inputs (small bonus)

When you level up:

* You gain stat points
* Your overall capability improves

---

2. SKILLS (MOST IMPORTANT)

Skills define your growth.

You unlock a skill the FIRST time you use it.

Examples:

* Use a sword → Swordsmanship
* Cast magic → Magic
* Move quietly → Stealth

To level skills:

* Use them consistently
* Use them effectively

IMPORTANT:

* Skills grow through repeated use
* Consistency matters more than variety

FOCUS = FASTER GROWTH

---

CLASSES

Your class is determined by your FIRST developed skill.

Examples:

* Swordsmanship → Swordsman
* Magic → Mage
* Stealth → Rogue
* Marksmanship → Hunter
* Defense → Guardian

You do not choose your class—the System assigns it based on your actions.

Advanced and higher classes unlock later through:

* Stat growth
* Continued specialization

---

AFFINITY (CRITICAL)

Your first core skill determines your affinity:

* Melee
* Magic
* Stealth
* Ranged
* Defense

Once set:

* Other core paths are limited
* Until Hybrid is unlocked

This makes early choices important.

---

HYBRID SYSTEM

Hybrid unlocks later through stat growth.

When unlocked:

* You can develop multiple paths
* Restrictions between skill types are removed

Hybrid builds are stronger but take longer to develop.

---

STAT ALLOCATION

Command:
allocate <stat> <amount>

Example:
allocate strength 3

Stats:

* Strength → physical power
* Agility → speed and movement
* Vitality → endurance
* Intelligence → magic and control
* Perception → awareness and reaction

Stats influence performance AND unlock higher progression.

HOW TO LEVEL FAST (IMPORTANT)

1. COMMIT EARLY
   Pick a style and stick with it.

2. BE SPECIFIC
   "I slash low and pivot back" is better than "I attack"

3. STAY CONSISTENT
   Repeated actions build skills quickly.

4. CONTROL YOUR ACTIONS
   Efficiency matters more than randomness.

5. USE YOUR STATS
   They unlock stronger paths and evolution.

---

EXAMPLE (GOOD)

"I step in, slash low, then follow with a quick upward strike."

→ Strong Swordsmanship growth

---

EXAMPLE (BAD)

"I attack, cast a spell, sneak, then shoot."

→ No clear progression

---

FINAL NOTE

It rewards:

* consistency
* control
* efficiency

What you repeat…
is what you become.
